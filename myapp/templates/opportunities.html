Training and Internships

NRICMI provides advanced training in molecular biology, bioinformatics, and medical material development, fostering skill development for students, clinicians, and researchers.

Thesis dissertation projects: Students can carry out their Bachelor’s or Master’s thesis dissertation projects at NRICMI. We support MSc and BSc thesis projects by providing mentorship, resources, and training. Students can engage in real-world research,contributing to ongoing projects while developing essential skills in experimental design, data analysis, and scientific communication.

Capacity Building Programs: Programs tailored for young scientists to build skills in data analysis, molecular research, and medical device development. Mentorships are offered to provide early exposure to research methods and innovative technologies.

Market Validation and Testing:help startups in validating their ideas and products through market research and focus groups through the support of Kerala Startup Mission and other Industry associations.

Services

Consultation Services: NRICMI offers specialized research services, such as whole genome sequencing, metagenome sequencing, and other sequencing-based services.

Analytical Services: NRICMI offers expert consultation in experimental design, data analysis, and result interpretation for researchers and startups in medical and life sciences.External researchers and entrepreneurs benefit from personalized guidance, promoting knowledge exchange and collaborative opportunities.

Support

Business Development Support: Provides mentorship programs, networking opportunities,and access to industry experts who can provide guidance on startup registration, collaborations, business connections, business strategy, etc.

Funding and Investment Support: Help startups get connected to various schemes under the State/Central Govt. BIIC organize pitching events, connects startups with potential investors, and provides guidance on preparing investment proposals and business plans.

Legal and Administrative Support:Connect the incubatees to legal and administrative authorities for tasks including company registration, intellectual property protection,licensing and compliance with regulations, etc.

Fellowships and Scholarships

NRICMI offers merit-based fellowships, including the prestigious “Dr. N Radhakrishnan Research Fellowship.”

Networking Opportunities for Researchers: The center organizes annual conferences on vascular health and collaborative research events to foster connections among scientists, clinicians, and industry experts.

Startup

Workspace for medical startups:NRICMI provides startups with physical office space,discussion/ meeting facilities and other necessary infrastructure.

Access to Expertise:Have a network of mentors, advisors, and industry experts who provide startups with specialized knowledge and guidance. These experts may offer one-on-one mentoring sessions, conduct workshops, or provide specific expertise in areas like technology, marketing, or finance.
